## üöÄ Getting Started

### Welcome to Bondarys!

Bondarys is your family's digital home - a comprehensive platform that keeps your family connected, safe, and organized. This guide will help you get started and make the most of all Bondarys features.

### Quick Start Guide

#### 1. Download and Install
- **iOS**: Download from the App Store
- **Android**: Download from Google Play Store
- **Web**: Access at https://bondarys.com

#### 2. Create Your Account
1. Open the Bondarys app
2. Tap "Create Account"
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile

#### 3. Set Up Your Family
1. Tap "Add Family Member"
2. Enter family member details
3. Send invitation via email or phone
4. Family member accepts invitation
5. Repeat for all family members

#### 4. Configure Safety Settings
1. Set up emergency contacts
2. Configure location sharing preferences
3. Create safety zones (geofences)
4. Set up emergency alerts

#### 5. Start Using Bondarys
- Share your location with family
- Send messages and make calls
- Use the family calendar
- Upload photos to family gallery

## üì± Core Features

### Location Sharing

#### Real-time Location Tracking
- **Enable Location**: Allow location access when prompted
- **Share Location**: Tap the location button to share your current location
- **View Family**: See all family members on the map
- **Location History**: View where family members have been

#### Location Settings
- **Always Share**: Share location continuously
- **Share When Using**: Share only when app is active
- **Manual Share**: Share location only when you choose
- **Never Share**: Don't share location at all

#### Privacy Controls
- **Family Only**: Location visible only to family members
- **Time Limits**: Set how long location is shared
- **Accuracy**: Choose location accuracy level
- **History**: Control location history retention

### Safety Features

#### Emergency Alerts
- **Panic Button**: Tap and hold for 3 seconds to send emergency alert
- **Emergency Contacts**: Alert sent to all emergency contacts
- **Location Included**: Your exact location is shared automatically
- **SOS Mode**: Continuous location sharing until deactivated

#### Geofencing
- **Create Zones**: Draw circles on the map to create safety zones
- **Enter/Exit Alerts**: Get notified when family members enter or leave zones
- **Custom Names**: Name zones (Home, School, Work, etc.)
- **Multiple Zones**: Create unlimited safety zones

#### Safety Zones
- **Safe Zones**: Areas where family members are safe
- **Danger Zones**: Areas to avoid
- **Temporary Zones**: Zones for specific events or trips
- **Zone Sharing**: Share zones with family members

### Family Communication

#### Messaging
- **Family Chat**: Group chat for all family members
- **Private Messages**: One-on-one conversations
- **Voice Messages**: Send voice recordings
- **Photo Sharing**: Share photos in conversations

#### Voice and Video Calls
- **Family Calls**: Group voice and video calls
- **Private Calls**: One-on-one calls
- **Screen Sharing**: Share your screen during calls
- **Call Recording**: Record important calls (with consent)

#### File Sharing
- **Photos**: Share family photos
- **Documents**: Share important documents
- **Videos**: Share family videos
- **Storage**: Secure cloud storage for all files

### Family Organization

#### Calendar
- **Family Events**: Add events for the whole family
- **Individual Events**: Personal events visible to family
- **Recurring Events**: Set up regular activities
- **Event Reminders**: Get notified before events

#### Photo Gallery
- **Family Photos**: Upload and organize family photos
- **Albums**: Create albums for different occasions
- **Sharing**: Share photos with family members
- **Backup**: Automatic backup of all photos

#### File Storage
- **Family Drive**: Secure storage for family files
- **Folders**: Organize files in folders
- **Sharing**: Share files with family members
- **Access Control**: Control who can access files

## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### App Won't Open
1. **Restart App**: Close and reopen the app
2. **Restart Device**: Restart your phone or tablet
3. **Update App**: Check for app updates
4. **Reinstall**: Delete and reinstall the app

#### Location Not Working
1. **Check Permissions**: Ensure location permission is granted
2. **Check GPS**: Make sure GPS is enabled
3. **Check Internet**: Ensure internet connection is working
4. **Check Settings**: Verify location settings in app

#### Messages Not Sending
1. **Check Internet**: Ensure internet connection is working
2. **Check Recipient**: Verify recipient is online
3. **Check Settings**: Verify messaging settings
4. **Restart App**: Close and reopen the app

#### Calls Not Working
1. **Check Microphone**: Ensure microphone permission is granted
2. **Check Internet**: Ensure stable internet connection
3. **Check Audio**: Verify audio settings
4. **Check Device**: Ensure device supports calls

### Error Messages

#### "Location Permission Required"
- **Solution**: Grant location permission in device settings
- **Steps**: Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Bondarys > Allow

#### "Internet Connection Required"
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection
- **Steps**: Connect to Wi-Fi or mobile data

#### "Account Verification Required"
- **Solution**: Verify your email address
- **Steps**: Check email and click verification link

#### "Family Invitation Expired"
- **Solution**: Request new invitation
- **Steps**: Ask family member to send new invitation

### Performance Issues

#### Slow Loading
1. **Check Internet**: Ensure fast internet connection
2. **Clear Cache**: Clear app cache in settings
3. **Restart App**: Close and reopen the app
4. **Update App**: Install latest app version

#### Battery Drain
1. **Location Settings**: Adjust location accuracy
2. **Background Activity**: Limit background app refresh
3. **Notifications**: Reduce notification frequency
4. **App Updates**: Keep app updated

#### Storage Issues
1. **Clear Cache**: Clear app cache
2. **Delete Old Files**: Remove old photos and files
3. **Upgrade Plan**: Consider premium plan for more storage
4. **Backup and Delete**: Backup important files and delete from app

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

#### What is Bondarys?
Bondarys is a comprehensive family safety and communication platform that helps families stay connected, safe, and organized through real-time location sharing, safety features, and family organization tools.

#### Is Bondarys free?
Bondarys offers a free plan with basic features. Premium features are available through subscription plans starting at $9.99/month.

#### Is Bondarys safe for children?
Yes, Bondarys is designed with children's safety in mind. We comply with COPPA regulations and provide parental controls for children under 13.

#### Can I use Bondarys internationally?
Yes, Bondarys is available worldwide and supports multiple languages including English, Thai, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean.

### Privacy and Security

#### Is my data secure?
Yes, all data is encrypted end-to-end and stored securely. We don't sell or share your data with third parties.

#### Who can see my location?
Only family members you authorize can see your location. You control all privacy settings.

#### Can I delete my data?
Yes, you can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

#### Is Bondarys GDPR compliant?
Yes, Bondarys fully complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations worldwide.

### Features and Functionality

#### How accurate is location tracking?
Location accuracy depends on your device and network. GPS provides the most accurate location data.

#### Can I create multiple family groups?
Currently, Bondarys supports one family group per account. We're working on multiple family group support.

#### Can I use Bondarys without location sharing?
Yes, you can use Bondarys for messaging and family organization without sharing your location.

#### How do emergency alerts work?
Emergency alerts send your location and emergency message to all emergency contacts. They can also contact emergency services if needed.

### Technical Support

#### How do I contact support?
You can contact support through the app, email at support@bondarys.com, or phone at [Phone Number].

#### What devices are supported?
Bondarys supports iOS 12+ and Android 8+ devices, as well as web browsers.

#### Can I use Bondarys on multiple devices?
Yes, you can use Bondarys on multiple devices with the same account.

#### How do I update the app?
App updates are available through the App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android).
