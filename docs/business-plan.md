## üí∞ Revenue Model

### Freemium Model

#### Free Tier
- **Features**:
  - Basic location sharing (5 family members)
  - Simple family chat
  - Emergency contacts (3 contacts)
  - Basic photo sharing (100 photos)
  - Family calendar (basic)
- **Limitations**:
  - Limited storage (1GB)
  - Basic support
  - Ad-supported
- **Conversion Rate Target**: 15%

#### Premium Tier ($9.99/month)
- **Features**:
  - Unlimited family members
  - Advanced location tracking
  - Video calls (unlimited)
  - Advanced safety features
  - Unlimited photo storage
  - Advanced calendar features
  - Priority support
  - Ad-free experience

#### Family Plan ($19.99/month)
- **Features**:
  - All Premium features
  - Family analytics
  - Advanced safety zones
  - Family health tracking
  - Educational content
  - Family counseling resources

#### Family Safety Solutions
- **Target**: Schools, organizations, communities
- **Pricing**: $5-10 per family/month

- **Features**: Direct integration, emergency protocols

- **Features**: Safety trends, location patterns
